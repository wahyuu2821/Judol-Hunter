Effective Date: 01/05/2025

1. Introduction

Judol Hunter ("we", "our", or "us") provides a service that moderates YouTube comments and removes those that indicate online gambling activity. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data, particularly when using services that interact with Google APIs.

By using our app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

  • Google Account Information: When you sign in using Google OAuth 2.0, we may collect your basic profile information, such as name, email address, and channel ID.
  • YouTube Comments: We access comments from your YouTube channel in order to analyze and moderate them.
  • Log Data: For security and analytics, we may log IP addresses, timestamps, and usage actions.

3. How We Use Information

We use your information solely for the following purposes:

  • To detect and remove YouTube comments that contain or promote online gambling content.
  • To display moderation statistics (e.g., number of removed comments).
  • To comply with Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

4. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share your information in limited circumstances:

  • With service providers (e.g., for hosting or database management) under strict data protection agreements.
  • If required by law or to protect our legal rights.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data. All communication with Google APIs is encrypted, and we follow industry best practices to secure our servers and data.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You may:

  • Revoke access at any time via your Google Account Permissions.
  • Request deletion of your stored data by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

This service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under this age.
